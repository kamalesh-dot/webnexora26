Competition Structure

5 Rounds of Excellence

Battle through 5 challenging rounds — 2 non-technical, 3 with eliminations. Only the best teams will reach the finals!

R1

Round 1: Meme Feast

Non-Technical

Kick off the competition with a hilarious meme challenge! Create the most relatable and creative memes. All teams participate — no eliminations. Show off your humor and team creativity!

10:00 AM - 11:00 AM (60 minutes)
R2

Round 2: Picture Maze

Non-Technical

Navigate your way through a visual puzzle challenge! Decode images, spot the differences, and unravel picture-based mysteries. Everyone continues — build momentum before the eliminations begin!

11:00 AM - 12:00 PM (60 minutes)
R3

Round 3: Quiz Mania

🔥 Elimination

First elimination! Test your knowledge in a fast-paced online quiz. Quick thinking and accuracy are key. Only top performers advance to Round 4.

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM (60 minutes)
R4

Round 4: Problem Pit

🔥 Elimination

Second elimination! Participants will be given a problem statement and without the help of any tools or devices, they must define the problem and find the solution using only their minds. Pure thinking, logic, and strategy will determine who moves forward to the final round.

2:10 PM - 3:10 PM (60 minutes)
R5

Round 5: Creative Arena

🏆 Grand Finale

The ultimate showdown! Unleash your creativity in this grand finale. Combine imagination, technical skill, and innovation to craft something extraordinary. The best team takes home the trophy!

3:20 PM - 4:00 PM (40 minutes)
